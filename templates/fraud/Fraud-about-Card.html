Fraud Occurence by Card Type

Based on the dataset, Debit Bank Cards were more likely to be the victimized by fraudulent transactions followed by Credit Card, and, then by Debit Prepaid Cards.

Fraudulent Cases:

  • Debit Cards 56.4%
  • Credit Cards 33.9%
  • Debit Prepaid Cards 9.7%

Fraud Occurence by Card Brand

Would the card with less fraudulent activity be deemed as the safest card? Based on a comparison of the Card Brands, Discover Cards had less activity while Master Card had more than 50% of fraudulent activities against their card.

  • Visa Card 36.4%
